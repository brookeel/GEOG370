Homework 6: Census Data Choropleth

by Brooke Elliott

This map is a choropleth that shows the percentage of unemployed citizens over 16 by county in New Mexico. The map in this image is in the EPSG:6528 projection, also known as New Mexico Central projection; it is a Transverse Mercator projection measured in meters. The data for this map was gathered in 2020 and uses American Community Survey 5-year estimates.
